Privacy

Private because it can't be otherwise.

Most "safety" apps upload your screen to a server to check it. SafeScreen checks everything on your phone — so your screen never has to leave it.

Your screen is never uploaded

The AI that checks your screen runs entirely on your phone's own chip. Each frame is analyzed on the spot and immediately discarded — no screenshots stored, nothing sent to a server.

Your account holds the minimum

Signing up takes an email — that's it. What appears on your screen is never tied to your account, tracked, or sold. There's simply no feed of your activity to leak.

No tracking, no ads

No analytics profile, no browsing history, no ad targeting. SafeScreen's only job is deciding blur-or-don't — and that decision never leaves the device.
